About

As a science teacher for 10 years, I am passionate about equal access to education. After seeing how distance learning affects the underprivileged population, I was inspired to get into software engineering. My first JavaScript project is dedicated to that mission: Octet Stabilizer, an educational game that reinforces the lesson on Chemistry's octet rule. Part of what got me into teaching in the first place is my love for learning, and I strive to bring that same passion for science to new technology.

As a full stack software developer, I enjoy using JavaScript, Ruby, React/Redux, Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL, HTML, and CSS/SCSS (nested syntax for the win). My most recent project, TagAlongs, is a MERN stack app that lets vulnerable members of their neighborhood request a volunteer to accompany them on their errands, utilizing the power of Google Maps API. I am especially proud of TagAlongs' mobile-friendly accessibility.
